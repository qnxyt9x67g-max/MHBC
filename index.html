Welcome

A Church That is Diligent in the Word. Determined in Our Walk - Delighted in Our Worship.

We believe that nothing is more important than someone’s relationship with Jesus. Our mission is to help people follow Jesus and to take their next step on their journey of faith. No matter where you are on that journey, we are here to walk beside you. We hope that at Maxwell Hill Baptist Church, you will discover a community of people just like you who will support, encourage, and love you as we follow Jesus.
